Budget Flow setup

Welcome to Budget Flow.

Budget Flow helps you track spending, stay ahead of your budget, and grow savings with a clearer view of your money across the app.

Track clearly

See transactions, categories, and monthly movement without losing the simple day-to-day view.

Budget confidently

Set spending limits, spot pressure early, and keep your reporting tied to clean stored values.

Save with intent

Build savings goals and keep progress readable in the currency view that feels natural to you.

How currency works: your first selection becomes your account currency for stored amounts. Later changes in Settings only change the display currency, and Budget Flow converts the view using market rates.

Choose your defaults

Pick the country, language, and currency you want Budget Flow to start with. You can adjust language and display currency later inside the app.

Your selected currency will be saved as your account currency for stored amounts. You can switch the display currency later without rewriting your data.
Exchange-rate display conversion uses market data from ExchangeRate-API.
